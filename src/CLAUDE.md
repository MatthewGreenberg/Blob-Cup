# src

`App.jsx` renders the baked `pinalty_stadium3.glb` (emissive/unlit, `toneMapped=false`) and surrounds it with procedural props styled to match the reference hero art (`src/assets/hero.png`):

- **Fan blob GLB** — `src/fan_blob_blender_script.py` builds/exports `public/fan_blob.glb` (178KB; 2026-07 cuteness pass: eyes+sparkles ×1.22, blush ×1.18 higher + rosier `#FF8FB4` projected flat onto the body ellipsoid (decal, was jutting out like arms), plus stubby nub arms in dusty pink `#D96E93` (instance tint multiplies, so arms render as a darker pink-shifted version of each fan's color; joined last — mind the single material slot or the export splits into 2 primitives) — script knobs match the edited `~/Desktop/Blend/blob.blend`): ONE mesh, one primitive, vertex colors (`COLOR_0`) for the face — white body takes `instanceColor` tint, dark eyes/mouth stay dark — plus 3 mouth morph targets (`MouthOpen`, `MouthWide`, `MouthFrown`). Intended to replace the canvas-face crowd via `InstancedMesh`: `setColorAt` (color), matrix Y-scale (height), `setMorphAt` (expression, r152+). Origin bottom-center, face on GLB +Z. The base is squashed flat with a slight outward bulge (contact patch, bottom re-seated at z=0) so blobs sit on the tiers instead of tangent-touching. Re-export: `Blender -b -P src/fan_blob_blender_script.py`.
- **Crowd** — one `InstancedMesh` (~320 instances) of the FanBlob GLB geometry with a fresh glossy `MeshPhysicalMaterial` (vertexColors, roughness 0.35, clearcoat 1 — the GLB's exported material is too matte for the candy-toy reference; the `Environment preset="city"` map feeds the clearcoat). Morphs work via `setMorphAt(i, proxyMesh)` — a throwaway `THREE.Mesh(geometry)` carries per-instance `morphTargetInfluences`, indices from `src.morphTargetDictionary`; set `mesh.morphTexture.needsUpdate` after the loop and do NOT strip `geometry.morphAttributes`. Palette is saturated candy brights (`FAN_COLS`), not pastels. Seeded (`mulberry32(11)`) so the arrangement is stable across renders (the react-hooks/purity lint rule also forbids `Math.random` in render/useMemo). Fans sit on tier tops mirroring the old Blender `BUILD_FANS` rig; side fans yaw ±π/2 toward the pitch (geometry faces work from any angle, unlike the old canvas-face decal), back fans face +Z. Per instance: `setColorAt` pastel, Y-scale height, `setMorphAt` expression mix (30% open / 30% wide / 8% frown / rest basis smile). `frustumCulled=false` (instance bounds ≠ single-blob geometry bounds). A second `InstancedMesh` of flat radial-gradient planes fakes per-fan contact shadows: the plane geometry is pre-rotated flat, so per-instance ellipse scale goes in **X/Z** (not Y); the gradient needs a near-opaque core because only the ring outside the ~0.68·scale blob base is ever visible; y-offset 0.06 above the tier (0.015 z-fights at this camera distance).
- **Player GLB** — `public/player.glb` (2.2MB): the Tripo-rigged chibi kicker from `~/Desktop/Blend/main-player.blend`, with four baked clips — `Kick` (1.7s one-shot: windup → contact → follow-through, right leg), `Celebrate` (2.5s one-shot: crouch → jump with arms up → land → sway), `Idle` (2s seamless breathing loop) and `Walk` (1s seamless in-place stride loop; the game translates the character). Animations are authored by `src/player_anim_blender_script.py` (headless: `Blender -b ~/Desktop/Blend/main-player.blend -P src/player_anim_blender_script.py -- --export`); it keyposes the rig via world-axis rotations (character faces +X in Blender), renders previews to the scratchpad, and exports armature+mesh only (ball Icosphere/camera/light excluded) as NLA tracks → one glTF clip each. Hip drops in crouch/land are matched to knee-bend leg shortening so feet stay on the floor plane. In-scene: `<Player />` (inside the stadium group) idles at `PLAYER_POS=[0,0,24]`, yaw +90° (model faces +X → faces the tunnel down -z), scale 3; drei `useAnimations` + leva 'player' panel buttons crossfade between clips; `Idle` autoplays, `Walk`/`Idle` loop, `Kick`/`Celebrate` are `LoopOnce` and hand back to `Idle` via the mixer's `finished` event. Materials are swapped for glossy `MeshPhysicalMaterial` (map/color kept, roughness 0.35, clearcoat 1 — same recipe as the crowd) and a contact-shadow plane inside the group uses `makePlayerShadowTexture` — a much stronger-cored gradient than the fans' (`makeShadowTexture(stops)` is the shared builder), since the player's shadow shows in full on bright grass while fan shadows only show as a thin ring.
- **Flag** — pennant cloths (`<Flag />`, vertex-waved planes) attached to the GLB's two flagpoles at GLB-local `(±10.6, z=-14/+10)` (the poles themselves ship in the GLB). The old pins and scoreboards components are gone.
- **Jumbotron** — `<Jumbotron />` (inside the stadium group) turns the baked `Scoreboard_Screen` into a live reverse-shot. A plane parked over the baked face (GLB-local `(0,13.41,-32.43)`, `26.06×8.32` — the Blender `Screen` box is 9.4×3.0 but the `RESIZE` pass scales the whole Scoreboard ×2.772 — tilted `+0.175` rad down at the pitch, inset ×0.92 so the baked glowing screen shows as a backlight bezel border; all leva-tunable in the `jumbotron` panel) shows a `WebGLRenderTarget` (`SRGBColorSpace`, 4× MSAA) rendered each frame from a second `perspectiveCamera` sitting behind the goal (`~(0,7,-17)`) looking back at the player (`~(0,2.5,22)`) — the opposite angle of the main pitch-level camera. `useFrame` hides the plane, `gl.render(scene, cam)` into the target, then restores it (feedback guard); the RT camera is a child of the group so its `lookAt` uses `plane.parent.localToWorld`. Runs at default priority so it renders before the `EffectComposer` (priority 1) writes the main view; the jumbotron image is thus one frame fresh and skips bloom/tonemapping (`meshBasicMaterial toneMapped=false`).
- **Sky** — a cloudless dusk-gradient sky sphere (warm tan horizon → grey-blue zenith, matching the bake's world lighting), sitting outside `<Center>` at scene root. Background/fog are the same warm haze. (Confetti and the HUD card were removed.)
- **Bloom** — `@react-three/postprocessing` `EffectComposer`/`Bloom` (mipmap blur, `luminanceThreshold=0.9`). Only the `toneMapped=false` emissives (scoreboards, confetti) push past threshold and glow; the ACES-tonemapped stadium stays clean.
- **Penalty game** — `<Game />` (inside the stadium group, GLB-local coords): aim with the pointer (yellow reticle rides a `Plane` raycast at the goal plane `GOAL_Z=-12.82` — the GLB's real goal frame line — clamped to a `±4.6 × 3.6` goal box), **hold left button to charge, release to shoot**. Phase machine `aim → charging → windup → flying → goal|rebound → aim` lives in `useState` (mounts/unmounts trail + reticle) with all motion in a `sRef` scratch object mutated only in handlers/`useFrame` (the react-compiler immutability rule rejects mutating `useMemo`/`useRef(...).current` values created in render — seed the ref from a `useLayoutEffect`; same rule forbids mutating `useAnimations`' `actions.*` in callbacks, so Player keeps its run-up state in a `walkRef` seeded from a `useLayoutEffect`). Charging: power ping-pongs 0.15↔1 over `CHARGE_TIME=0.9s` (emitted as `stadium:power` every frame → HUD writes a `--p` CSS var directly, no re-render); holding also **runs the player up**: Player jogs (Walk clip at 1.6× while the game lerps `group.position.z`) from `PLAYER_POS z=24` to `PLAYER_KICK_Z=20` over `RUN_UP_TIME=0.85s` and idles at the ball if the hold outlasts the approach. Release kicks — if he's still mid-approach the walk finishes first (release retargets the walk's `arrive` to 'kick'), so Game's launch delay is dynamic: `shot.windupWait = max(0, RUN_UP_TIME - holdTime) + KICK_CONTACT` (`KICK_CONTACT=0.66s` = foot-on-ball, frame 16 @ 24fps; `shot.t` is zeroed on pointerdown so it doubles as hold time). When the round resets, Game emits `stadium:reset` and Player turns to camera and trots back to the mark (`PLAYER_RETURN_TIME=1.1s`) before facing the goal again; starting a new charge mid-return cancels the walk-back and the next run-up starts from wherever he stands (fixed duration, variable stride). Power scales flight time (1.1s lob → 0.5s rocket), arc, rebound punch, trail size, flame puff size — and the keeper's reach grows as power drops, so weak shots get saved. Releasing inside the **perfect zone** (`PERFECT_MIN=0.86`–`PERFECT_MAX=0.97`, drawn as a gold band on the HUD power bar) is an unsaveable strike: `stadium:perfect` event, PERFECT! banner, white-hot second trail, bigger/more fire puffs, hotter ball emissive. Keeper: giant green fan blob (same `fan_blob.glb` geometry as the crowd, non-instanced — call `mesh.updateMorphTargets()` in a layout effect or morph influences don't exist) guarding the goal line at `GOAL_Z`. While you aim/charge he **shuffles the goal line** (`sin(t·1.7)·2.0`, leaning into his movement via `rotation.z`); on release 35% of the time he reads your aim side, otherwise he **commits to his current lean** (center if `|keeperX| < 0.8`) — so the core skill is watching his shuffle and shooting the other way. Dive lerps from the live shuffle x to `diveX` by dive progress, with damp+tilt, `MouthWide` scream, frown when scored on; base save window `|aimX-diveX| < 1.6 && aimY < 3.0` (full-power high corners always score, perfect strikes always score). The ball sits at `BALL_START z=17.9`, ~31 units from the goal. Fire: two layered drei `<Trail>`s (widths/lengths scale with the frozen `shotPower` state; `attenuation w³`) + a `FIRE_POOL=56` `InstancedMesh` of blob-spheres spawned at the ball while it's fast, shrinking `(1-k²)` along a white→yellow→orange→ember `lerpColors` ramp, plus emissive on the ball while in flight; trails only mount while the ball is loose so reset teleports don't streak. OrbitControls: `LEFT: -1` / one-finger `-1` (left is the charge button), rotate on right-drag / two fingers. Components stay decoupled via window `stadium:windup/power/kick/perfect/goal/save/reset` CustomEvents (`emit` + `useStadiumEvent` helpers): Player runs up/kicks/celebrates/walks back, Crowd flips to celebration for 4.5s on goal, and the DOM `<Hud />` (outside the Canvas, `App.css` `.game-*`) tracks goals/shots plus a 🔥 goal **streak** (shown at ≥2, folded into the GOOOAL! banner at ≥3, reset by saves), shows the power bar (with gold perfect zone) while charging, and pops the GOOOAL!/SAVED!/PERFECT! banner.

Layout note: props live **inside the stadium group** (fixed `STADIUM_POS=[0, 0.3, 3.0]` offset — grounds/centers the GLB's y -0.3 / z -39..33 bounds; no `<Center>`, which re-measures animated rigs) in GLB-local coords. Blender is Z-up, GLB is Y-up, so Blender `(x,y,z)` → three `(x, z, -y)`. Placement mirrors the current bake source `src/stadium_blender_script.py` (`LANE_W=18`, `LANE_L=46`, side tiers at `x=side*(12+2.4t)`, tier-top `z=1.4+1.1t`, a 9u-tall cream perimeter wall (mint trim cap) behind the bleachers at `x=±21` and behind the goal end at `y=37` (camera end open, no pitch-side walls), tunnel front at `y≈22.8` (crown removed), a white `Goal` frame (`9.6w × 3.9h`, posts ±4.8) at `y=12.82` — net ships as a three.js layer, flagpoles at `(±10.6, 14/-10)`, back-pair floodlights only, plus dressing: penalty box/goal-line markings (penalty-spot marker removed), candy ad hoardings at `x=±10.4`, a pink wall accent band, and a glowing scoreboard on the back wall above the tunnel). Fans and flag cloths are not built there — they are three.js layers (the fan-rig logic has been removed from `stadium_blender_script.py`). `EXPORT_GLB=True` bakes COMBINED lighting (linear bake → sRGB PNG via view transform → emissive-only material) and writes `public/pinalty_stadium3.glb`; the white `Line_*` pitch markings are excluded from the bake (their tiny UV islands baked black) and export as a second `Lines` mesh with a flat emissive-white material; three.js rules: `SRGBColorSpace`, `toneMapped=false`. The old `script.py` (`LANE_W=4`) is superseded.

Camera is a low pitch-level one-point view down the lanes at the tunnel (matches the Blender hero render): `fov=42`, pos `(0, 8, 28)`, target `(0, 2.2, -14)`. Scene lighting is dusk-under-floodlights — dim warm key + hazy tan fog/sky — and only affects the props; the stadium itself is baked/unlit. **Gotcha:** the stadium offset is the hand-tuned `STADIUM_POS`, so a re-exported GLB with a different footprint needs that constant retuned (y = -bounds.min.y, z = -(bounds.min.z+bounds.max.z)/2).
